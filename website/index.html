Leveling the playing field

What We Offer

Everything a modern farmer needs

Pricing Intelligence

Real-time crowdsourced prices for crops, fuel, fertilizer, and chemicals — submitted by farmers in your region and updated throughout the day.

  • Crop prices — Corn, Soybeans, Canola, Wheat & more
  • Fuel & input cost tracking (fertilizer, chemicals)
  • Filter by country, province / state & district
  • Anonymous submissions — no account required

Marketplace

One platform connecting the entire farming community — custom services, equipment, land, and employment all in one place.

Custom Farming Job Board

Post jobs or find operators for seeding, spraying, harvesting, and more.

  • Tilling, Spraying, Seeding, Combining, Swathing & more
  • Browse operators by service, region & availability
  • Post a job or submit a quote directly in the app
  • Message operators and track job status

Buy / Sell — Equipment & Land

List or find farming equipment, cropland, pasture, and mixed farm properties.

  • Buy & sell farm equipment, parts & machinery
  • Farmland listings — for sale or rent
  • Cropland, pasture & mixed farm properties
  • Direct seller contact — no middleman fees

Farmhands — Employment Postings

Post or find seasonal and full-time farm employment across your region.

  • Post or find full-time, seasonal & casual farm work
  • Upload a resume and apply to jobs with one tap
  • Browse by region & employment type
  • Manage applications and communicate in the app
