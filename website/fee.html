Pricing & Access

Built on Trust,
Not Paywalls

FarmingPal is a community-powered platform. While we establish and verify the quality standards our services are held to, the app remains accessible at no charge.

Current Status

No charges while quality standards are being established

FarmingPal is in an active growth phase. We are building out our marketplace services — custom farming operators, equipment listings, land sales, and farm employment — and setting the quality benchmarks that every service provider on the platform is expected to meet.

Until those standards are confirmed and consistently met, we are not charging for access to any part of the platform. Farmers can browse prices, submit reports, post jobs, and connect with operators without a subscription or upfront fee.

Our Commitment

Crowdsourced pricing will always be accessible

The core of FarmingPal is community price reporting — farmers sharing what they see at the elevator, the fuel pump, and the co-op so that every grower has access to the same market intelligence that large agribusinesses take for granted.

Regardless of any future pricing changes to premium services, crowdsourced price browsing and submission will remain accessible to every farmer on the platform. That is the foundation this community is built on, and we will not change it.

Looking Ahead

What may change — and how we'll handle it

As the marketplace grows, certain premium features may carry a fee. These could include priority placement for service providers, verified operator badges, advanced analytics, or tools designed specifically for commercial operators and agribusinesses.

Any fee changes will be communicated well in advance. We will never silently introduce charges to features that were previously accessible, and existing users will always receive appropriate notice before any billing begins.

Questions about pricing?

If you have specific questions about how pricing may affect your use of FarmingPal, reach out directly — we are happy to explain where things stand.
